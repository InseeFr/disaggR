# Total consumption in accommodation and food services at current prices

An annual time series containing the consumption in accommodation and
food services services from 1999 to 2021, as retrieved the 04/07/2022.

## Usage

``` r
data(consumption_catering)
```

## Format

A time series of frequency 1
