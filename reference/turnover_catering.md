# Turnover indicator in accommodation and food services

An monthly time series containing the turnover indicator in
accommodation and food services from january 1999 to april 2022, as
retrieved the 04/07/2022.

## Usage

``` r
data(turnover_catering)
```

## Format

A time series of frequency 12
